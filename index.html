Rose-Hulman Linux Users Group

Email Bounce

Join our email bounce to get updates about meetings and stuff. Note that to change your bounce settings you must be on campus.

Wiki

We maintain a Wiki for people using Linux at Rose-Hulman along with other general Linux information.

VPN

To access our physical machines you have to use our SSL VPN. This will also allow to access on-campus only resources from off campus. For more info visit our Wiki.

Nextcloud

Nextcloud is an open source and self-hosted alternative to Dropbox (and a few other things) which we host for Rose-Hulman Students, Faculty, and Staff to use. Us hosting this is still very munch in alpha/beta, email the sysadmin for an account.

Physical/Virtual Machines

We have a general use Linux machine for people to run stuff on, along with bare metal servers as well as virtual machines that people can request. Visit our Wiki page for updated information on availability and how to get one.

About

The Rose-Hulman Linux Users Group exists objective is: to introduce alternative operating systems to anyone interested; to provide hardware and software to allow advanced users to continue learning from each other; to support members of the Rose-Hulman community with issues related to Free and Open Source Software; to encourage the use of Free Software and Open Standards; and to inform and discuss advancements in computing technology.
